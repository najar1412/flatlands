Story Time

Hello, I'm Rory! Technical Director of a creative agency based in New york where we predominantly produce computer generated still and film visualisations of Architecture both built and unbuilt.

My week to week consists of the development of tools and services that improve throughput and efficiency of our stills and film teams. I handle the administration and implementation of our studios pipeline as well as exploring new technologies we can potentially use in the delivery of client products.

Outside of my day job I enjoy building things. The things i like to build vary. Whether its 3d rendered imagery user tools and plugins or software systems its all on a computer. I spend a fair amount of my time on a computer and to offset all this sitting i hike. I like to plan a route, pack a bag and hit the closest most isolating forest for a few nights.
